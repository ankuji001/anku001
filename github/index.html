ABOUT UTSAV

We think that it is about manifesting, it is about memories which are created ! Utsav is a blend of technical
,non-technical and cultural events that will definitely give an empyrean experience.
It is a perfect coalesce of fun and frolic of excitement and ecstacy, of devotion and dedication.
The fest is dedicated to blossom the young artists from all over the country a ideal platform.

UTSAV is a vibrant celebration of student creativity and spirit,
brimming with music, dance, and colorful activities.
It’s a time when friendships are forged, and unforgettable memories are made.
Amidst the excitement, it’s also a chance to showcase talents
and enjoy the collective energy of the campus community.

So why wait? Register now for UTSAV 2024

Whether you're a student, professional, or just someone who loves technology and culture, UTSAV 2024 is the place to be this year. With a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere, exciting competitions, and plenty of opportunities to learn and grow, UTSAV 2024 is the perfect way to kick-start your journey towards a brighter and more creative future. See you there!
